## Fixture Details

- Main wraps call in try/catch with `std.try`

### 172_async_nested_await
- Creates `inner` async (adds 10 to input)
- Creates `outer` async (calls inner)
- Main calls outer with 5, gets 5+10=15
- Expected output: "15"

### 173_async_multiple_futures
- Creates `asyncDouble` (multiply by 2)
- Creates `asyncTriple` (multiply by 3)
- Main calls both with different values: double(10)=20, triple(5)=15
- Adds results: 20+15=35
- Expected output: "35"
